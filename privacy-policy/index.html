Privacy Policy for Khayyam App

Information We Collect

Khayyam is an offline app that provides Omar Khayyam poems to users. The App does not collect any personal information from users directly. However, we use Crashlytics and Analytics services provided by Firebase to collect anonymous usage data and crash reports for the purpose of improving app performance and user experience. This data may include information such as device information, usage statistics, and performance metrics. We do not link this information to any personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected through Crashlytics and Analytics is used solely for the purpose of analyzing app usage patterns, identifying and fixing app crashes, and improving overall app functionality and performance. We do not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose this information to third parties.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through our App. Access to this information is restricted to authorized personnel only, and we use industry-standard security measures to safeguard against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes we make to this Privacy Policy will be posted on this page, and the revised Privacy Policy will indicate the date it was last updated. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
